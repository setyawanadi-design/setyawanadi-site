---
title: "Designing for Spatial Awareness"
date: 2024-01-05
category: Review
---

Spatial design in digital interfaces is about creating a mental model for the user. Where are they in the hierarchy? How did they get here? Where can they go next?

By utilizing overlaps, shadows, blurring (like iOS frosted glass), and consistent animation curves, we help users understand the physical relationship between digital components.

For example, a modal sliding up from the bottom implies it sits *on top* of the current context, and should be dismissing by swiping it back down into the void it came from.

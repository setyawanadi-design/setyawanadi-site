---
title: "The Joy of Mechanical Keyboards"
date: 2023-11-05
category: Note
---

There is something inherently satisfying about the tactile feedback of a good mechanical keyboard. It’s not just about typing speed or gaming performance; it’s about the physical connection between thought and digital expression.

The sharp clack of a Blue switch or the muted thock of a padded linear switch can completely change the atmosphere of a workspace. 

I recently spent a weekend lubing the switches on my Keychron Q1, and the difference is night and day. It turns an ordinary peripheral into a crafted tool.
